Welcome

BUYING A HOME SELLING A HOME
Bios are traditionally written in the third-person; however, I have chosen to present this bio in the first-person to underscore my determination to always be there for you in person from beginning to end. How do you define success? For me, success in real estate means repeat business from past home sellers and buyers, underscored by referrals to their friends, family and associates. There are no tricks - just an upfront approach based on a thorough knowledge of the Vancouver market, a firm grasp of technology, a strong work ethic, my obsession with personal service and a total commitment to my clients.

I have sold hundreds of homes since 2000. While my achievements have been recognized in the Hall Of Fame and through other peer awards recognizing top performing REALTORS, it is the kind words from clients that mean the most. I believe that my professional and personable approach will help to reduce your stress while I work to obtain the best price and terms when selling or buying real estate for you.
