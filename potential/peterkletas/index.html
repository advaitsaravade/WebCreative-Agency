Welcome

Thank you for visiting PeterKletas.com

I understand that for most people, Buying and selling of real estate can be an overwhelming experience. There are just too many factors to consider all at once. My role as I see it, is to act as your personal facilitator to make the process as smooth as possible. Being current with market trends and having a clear understanding of what my client wants are key elements to a successful experience. Listening plays a big part in the whole process and I like to start things off by listening to what you, the client, want.

Born and raised in Vancouver and a graduate of the Urban Land Economics program at UBC, I have gotten to know the various neighbourhoods of this exceptional city quite well over the years. As a recipient of Sutton Group’s Platinum Award, I take pride in doing a good job and, more importantly, in earning your trust.

The Newport

3480 Main Street, Vancouver V5V 3N2

Located in the popular Main St. corridor, walking distance to good coffee shops, restaurants, parks and schools. The Newport is a 164 unit wood frame complex constucted in 1993. In 2001, the strata corporation took the difficult and costly decision to re-do the entire building envelope, implementing a rainscreen barrier throughout the exterior surface. Upon completion, the strata purchased a 10 year warranty on the rainscreen surface.
